Drive controls:

The Thrustmaster joystick will be used for driving.

Drive system will be Mechanum using CTRE Phoenix Framework Software for drive controls.

X axis will move the robot left and right.

Y axis will move the robot forward and backward.

Z rotation will rotate the robot.


Fire controls:

The Xbox controller will be used for loading and firing the balls.

Left bumper/trigger will handle load/intake operations.

Right bumper/trigger will handle launch/fire operations.

The B button will allow the controller to brake the drive motors in preparation for launch.


Arm controls:

The Xbox controller will be used for loading and firing the balls.

The D-pad Up button will expand the arm out.

The D-pad Down buttonw will contract/lower the arm down.

The arm motors will always operate at maximum speed.
